WHAT IS MOTSDRAMA.COM? AND WHAT'S WITH THAT NAME, HUH?

well first of all, stop yelling. it's giving me a headache. second of all, motsdrama.com is a host for all of my creative endeavors, ideas, projects, thoughts, discoveries, and much, MUCH more. as for the name, it refers to a phenomenon known to happen in a favorite game of mine, Shin Megami Tensei: Nocturne. near the end of the game (no spoilers, of course) you fight a boss named Mot. he has the ability to give himself infinite turns in battle using a move called Beast Eye. think of it like a "using wishes to wish for more wishes" sort of deal. this leads to a difficult and often comedic battle where the boss will give himself a ton of turns, and then wipe the floor with your party. the Japanese community dubbed this occurence...

"モト劇場"

(Mot's Theater, or Mot's Drama)

since it's like he's the one running the entire battle. now, this leads to the next obvious question. why did i name my website that? well, the real answer is that i thought that was a really cool sounding name and thought it'd make for a good username, but i already have an established username online. so i decided to give it to this website instead.
